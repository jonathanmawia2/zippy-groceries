About Fruits

"Fruits are an essential part of a healthy diet, providing a rich source of vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They come in a wide variety of flavors, colors, and textures, making them both delicious and nutritious. Many fruits, such as oranges, strawberries, and kiwis, are packed with vitamin C, which helps boost the immune system. Others, like bananas and avocados, are excellent sources of potassium, supporting heart health and muscle function."
